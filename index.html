Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

From my understanding, Progressive Enhancement is a way of development that ensures users with non-mainstream level devices still get all the same content. Websites following progressive enhancement are built from the ground up to ensure all content is accessible by devices. On the other end of the spectrum, Graceful Degradation is starts at the highest level of devices and compatibility/accessibility is only ensured later in development.

I believe Progressive Enhancement is a very important path of development. Clients will typically want their content seen before any fanciful gimmicks. Despite how high tech most devices have become, not everyone will have devices capable of javascript gimmicks and what not - with users potentially missing out on swaths of content.
